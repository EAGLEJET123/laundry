Location FAQs

Common questions about our locations

Do all locations offer the same services?

Yes, all our locations offer the full range of FreshClean NG services including wash & fold, dry cleaning, ironing, and traditional attire care. Some locations may have additional specialized equipment for specific treatments.

Can I drop off laundry at any location?

Absolutely! You can drop off your laundry at any of our locations regardless of where you placed your order. Our system tracks all orders across locations.

Do you offer pickup and delivery service?

Yes, we offer free pickup and delivery service within a 10km radius of each location. For areas beyond this radius, a small delivery fee may apply.

Are your locations open on public holidays?

Our locations operate on reduced hours during public holidays. Please check our website or contact your nearest location for specific holiday hours.

Can I track my order from any location?

Yes, all orders are tracked through our central system. You can monitor the status of your laundry through our mobile app or website regardless of which location is processing it.
