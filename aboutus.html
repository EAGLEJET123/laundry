About FreshFold

Nigeria's Premier Laundry Service - Revolutionizing Fabric Care Since 2020

Our Story

From Humble Beginnings to Industry Leaders

FreshFold NG was founded in 2020 by Chinedu Okonkwo, an Edo state entrepreneur who noticed the growing need for reliable, high-quality laundry services in Nigeria's bustling cities. What started as a small operation with two washing machines in Upper has grown into Nigeria's most trusted laundry service with facilities in Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt.

Our journey has been fueled by a commitment to excellence and a passion for making life easier for busy Nigerians. We've invested in state-of-the-art German cleaning technology while maintaining the personal touch that sets us apart from competitors.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional laundry services that save time, protect fabrics, and exceed customer expectations through innovative solutions and eco-friendly practices.

Our Vision

To become Nigeria's most trusted household service brand, expanding our reach to every major city while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
